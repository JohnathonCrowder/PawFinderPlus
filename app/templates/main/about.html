Our Story

PuppyHorizon was born out of a passion for dogs and a vision to transform the breeding industry. Our journey began in 2020 when our founder, a veteran dog breeder, recognized the need for a comprehensive, user-friendly solution to manage breeding programs effectively.

What started as a simple idea has grown into a robust platform serving thousands of breeders worldwide. We've combined cutting-edge technology with deep industry knowledge to create a tool that not only simplifies breeding management but also promotes responsible breeding practices.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our offerings, always with the goal of supporting breeders in their quest to produce healthy, happy puppies and advance the quality of dog breeds worldwide.

Key Features

Advanced Genetic Tracking

Utilize cutting-edge algorithms to monitor and analyze genetic health, ensuring optimal breeding pairs and healthier litters.

Comprehensive Breeding Calendar

Stay organized with our intuitive scheduling system, tracking heat cycles, breeding dates, and whelping timelines with ease.

Health Record Management

Maintain detailed health records for each dog, including vaccinations, medications, and vet visits, all in one centralized location.
